How is our veterinary clinic different from others?

The private veterinary center "Friend" was founded in Kyiv back in 1996. The first steps were timid, but over 28 years of responsible work we have the main thing - recognition and trust of our clients. Our medical methods and approaches, management style and level of service are an example for colleagues throughout the country, which they are happy to use. What are our advantages: 24/7 operation without days off - we are ready to provide veterinary care at any time; reception is by appointment, so there is enough time for the patient and we do not have queues; we have an institution of the attending physician - this means that the condition of your pet will be monitored by a personal veterinarian, who will know its history and will be able to take best care of the health of the animal; the head physician takes part in the process, monitors the quality of services; modern technological equipment of the clinics: premium ultrasound machines, digital X-ray, equipment for electrocardiogram and ECHO, computer and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) for accurate diagnostics of diseases of the spine, brain, lungs, bones, etc. operating rooms are equipped with all the necessary equipment to perform operations of any complexity. laboratory tests are performed by the reputable laboratory "Bio Soft", and the quality of the tests is controlled using the European quality control program Prev–µcal. Our clients receive the test results by mail.
